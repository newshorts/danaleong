The TEKTONIK initiative led by award-winning sonic architect Dana Leong unites artists around the globe who come from areas affected by major natural disasters. The core quartet is made of Dana Leong from China and Japan - cello, composer, Neel Murgai from India - sitar, Sameer Gupta from India - tabla, and DJ Val-Inc from Haiti - turntables, electronics.
